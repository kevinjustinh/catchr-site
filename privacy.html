Privacy Policy

Catchr Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Catchr is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers track applications and tailor resumes using AI. This policy explains what data Catchr accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected.

Data We Never Collect

Catchr has no backend server. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have any access to your personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following is stored in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device:

  • Your Gemini API key
  • Your Google Doc resume ID(s)
  • Your Google Sheet ID (for job tracking)

Data Sent to Third-Party Services

Catchr connects to the following Google services on your behalf using your own credentials:

  • Google Sheets API — to log job applications to your personal Google Sheet
  • Google Docs API — to read your resume and create tailored copies in your Google Drive
  • Google Drive API — to organize tailored resume copies into a folder in your Drive
  • Google Gemini API — your resume content and the job description are sent to Gemini using your own API key to generate tailored edits and fit scores

All Google API access is authorized through Google OAuth and governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Your Gemini API key is used directly from your browser — it is not routed through any Catchr server.

Job Page Data

When you use Catchr on a job listing page, the extension reads the page content (job title, company name, location, and job description) to pre-fill fields and power AI features. This data is processed locally or sent directly to Google's Gemini API. It is not stored or transmitted anywhere else.

Permissions

Catchr requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • identity — to authenticate with your Google account via OAuth
  • storage — to save your settings locally in your browser
  • activeTab / scripting — to read job listing pages when you open the extension
  • tabs — to open tailored resume documents in new tabs
  • notifications — to notify you when a resume has finished tailoring

Children's Privacy

Catchr is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
